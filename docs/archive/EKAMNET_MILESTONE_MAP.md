## 2. Milestone Evolution & Reorganization Chronicles

### Merge of Milestone 4 (Structured Proposition Compilation) into Milestone 3
*   *Original Plan*: Milestone 4 was designed to implement the post-hoc structured translation adapter (Strategy A) to generate structured propositions from unstructured theories.
*   *Modification Reason*: Bounded engineering spikes on llama3.2 (DEC_005) proved that single-stage LLM translation failed compliance thresholds. We resolved this by implementing prompt-decomposed **Sequential structured formation (Calls 1 & 2)**, which natively generated structured theories and sibling candidates. This bypassed the need for an independent post-hoc adapter, merging Milestone 4 directly into the Milestone 3 Sibling Generation pipeline (DEC_007).

### Deferral of Milestone 8 (Deferred Candidate Re-entry)
*   *Original Plan*: Milestone 8 was scheduled immediately after Milestone 7 (Minimal Causal Learning Loop) to validate the recovery and re-entry of previously retired theories.
*   *Modification Reason*: Causal loop audits (EF-004) proved that trigger-pruning learning caused catastrophic collapse (-76.92% selection rate) in context-shift regimes due to overgeneralization. We concluded that candidates cannot be safely reactivated or evaluated for re-entry without a deterministic feedback channel. Milestone 8 was deferred pending the design and implementation of the **Milestone 9 Proposition Validation Engine** (providing the required `ValidationRecord` database feedback).
